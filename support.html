Before You Reach Out

To help us assist you as quickly as possible, please include the following in your email:

  • The name of the app you need help with
  • Your device and operating system version (e.g., iPhone 15, iOS 18)
  • A brief description of the issue or question
  • Any screenshots that may help illustrate the problem

Common Topics

  • Account & billing: Questions about subscriptions, purchases, or restoring transactions
  • Bug reports: Something not working as expected? Let us know and we'll look into it
  • Feature requests: Have an idea to improve one of our apps? We're always listening
  • General inquiries: Anything else — we're happy to help
